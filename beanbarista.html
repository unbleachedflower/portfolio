Beans & Barista
Mobile iWatch App Design

Overview

For all the coffee lovers who would love to learn more about home brewing coffee methods, Bean & Barista Mobile/iWatch app is the one-stop-shop for them to try out numerous ways of coffee brewing in the comfort of their home. Apart from video instructions, users can also purchase their beloved coffee making appliances directly from the app.

01  Problem

There's no one-stop-shop for coffee lovers to explore more possibilities with coffee brewing recipes or purchase editor's pick artisan coffee products like beans or brewing appliances.

02  Competitive Analysis

Very limited, none-robust receipes that hardly get updated.

No option for customers to purchase directly in the app.

No iWatch app to better guide instructions.

03  User Persona

Based on user research analysis, we created a persona to understand what a potential customer of the app would be. We identified her goal, interests, and pains.

04  Solution

“An mobile app accompanied by an iWatch app providing all the latest instructions on coffee brewing methods. The app will incorporate the e-commerce functionality so that users can buy directly from the app.”

05  User Roadmap

Initial roadmap was drawn to ensure the streamlined user experience. This also helps determine the site information structure and backend data schema.

06  Wireframe

Wireframes were then discussed, drawn, and agreed upon for the entire user flow. Users will be able to choose their preferred brewing methods based on the taste of the coffee. Video instructions will be available after the user clicks on a specific brewing method tab. After the video, users can scroll up and see detailed instructions for each step. They can also select and add any tools they like from the recipe directly to their shopping carts.

07  Prototype

“ We applied logic between the pages according to our user roadmap, added missing pages, deleted redundant content. "

08  Style Tile

We want to emphasize the "artisan" character of the app. Therefore, we made sure that our color palette conveys sophistication and craftsmanship.

To add more of a vintage aesthetic, we chose the serif font "Made Mirage" as our main typography. We also selected a san-serif font "Rounded Elegance" as our second typography to strike a balance in design.
