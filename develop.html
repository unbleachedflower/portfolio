Space Voyager
Overview

An interactive mobile game with a storyline in space.

You finished mission #890 Moon Mission and ready to go home. But you are trapped in the dark side of the moon. You need to avoid all asteroids to escape to the bright side of the moon, then collect all falling stars to fly back home. Good Luck space voyager!

Technology Used

HTML, CreateJs, VanillaJs, Adobe Illustrator

Overview
