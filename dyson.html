dyson on: Digital Magazine
Website/Mobile Design

Overview

In early 2019, dyson started to adapt its print magazine - dyson on: to digital platforms, in the hope that this effort could help the company to reach to younger demographics.

I was appointed as the UX designer for the magazine redesign. Meanwhile, I also planned and executed a strategic digital marketing plan for the outreach effort.

dyson on: won the 2019 BEING DYSON AWARD for its outstanding design and social impact.

01  Background

As a fast growing engineering company, dyson has plenty to talk about. To share insights, the company runs dyson on: - a quarterly published magazine that tells design, technology, and engineering stories.

02  Problem

The once prominent printed magazine needed to be redesigned for the website and mobile interfaces.

The goal of this design project is to attract a younger audience with an interactive user experience. dyson assembled a global team to achieve this goal.

03  Market Research

I went through several rounds of market research to find the right media platforms for the magazine. Based on the magazine's tonality and content, we decided to launch its global version on Medium and Instagram and to launch its Chinese version on WeChat and Zhihu.

04  Solution

“Redesign & Reiterate dyson on: for an interactive digital experience.”

05  Design Style Tile

I inherited dyson's official "Futura BT Font for Dyson" as the standard English typography and selected "方正大黑简体" as the main typography for the Chinese content. As for the color scheme, I chose the colors that can best present Dyson's strengths in design, engineer, and technology.

I would like to convey the magazine's modern and sleek aesthetic to readers. Therefore, I designed the main logo in geomatrical shapes.

06  Wireframes

I sketched out the wireframes for different platforms. For the Medium site, I identified the layout of the main homepage, which would host various issues of the magazine, and the category pages, which would display the content by topics. For the WeChat platform, I discussed with the team and settled on initial user flow.

07  Site Information Structure

Then I applied the logic between pages, added missing pages, and deleted redundant content.

10  Usability Testing

I interviewed twelve young dyson on: readers for design feedback. Overall speaking, they were very impressed by the modern style of the layout but suggested that the information structure can be further simplified. I made adjustments to the information structure accordingly.
