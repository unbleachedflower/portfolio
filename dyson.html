Dyson on: Digital Magazine
Website
Mobile Design

Overview

To embrace digital transformation and reach directly to the target audience, Dyson made an effort to adapt its print magazine - Dyson on: to digital platforms. The magazine was optimized for user experience on both desktop and mobile interfaces. Social media marketing was also incorporated to provide users with integrated digital product experience. Dyson on: project won the 2019 Dyson Award for its outstanding content and social engagements.

01  Background

As a fast growing technology engineering company, Dyson has plenty to talk about. To share the most important behind the scene news, stories, the company runs Dyson on: - a quarterly publish magazine that delivers design, technology, and engineering topic stories.

02  Problem

With the trend of media digitalization and the rise of social media, the once pronounced printed magazine needed to be redesigned.

The question "How to attract its target audience and deliver an optimized user experience" is at the core of this project. An global effort was made to solve the problem.

03  Market Research

We went through several rounds of the digital media landscape research. Given the tonality and content of the magazine we decided on launching the magazine digital expereince on Medium and Instagram for global markets along with WeChat and Zhihu for China market.

04  Solution

“Redesign & Reiterate Dyson on: magazine for a streamlined digital experience.”

05  Design Style Tile

We inherited Dyson's official font as the standard English typography and selected 方正大黑简体 as the main typography for the Chinese content. As for the color theme, we chose the colors that can best present Dyson's design, engineer, and technology characters.

We would like to convey a modern, sleek design style to readers for the magazine. Therefore, we chose a set of smooth geomatrical shape as the main logo design.

06  Wireframe

We discussed and sketched out wireframe for different platforms. For the Medium site, we identified the layout of the main homepage that host different issues of the magazine and the category pages that display content by topic. For the WeChat platform, we agreed on the userflow of a mobile experience.

07  Site Information Structure

“ We applied logic between the pages according to our user roadmap, added missing pages, deleted redundant content. "

09  Usability Testing

We sat down with around 12 different Dyson owners for all four platforms and asked them for the first round of design feedbacks. Overall speaking, they were very impressed by the modern and clean style of the layout but suggested that the information structure can be further simplied and messages can be more straight forward. We made adjustment accordingly.
